What is most neog camp?

17th December,2020

Neog camp is a 4-month web development bootcamp. The structure of this camp is divided into two parts.

i)level zero
i)level one

Students will be selected to level one based on their performance in level zero. The fun part about camp is that here we learn concepts by building things contrary to the traditional way of learning things and trying to build things on top of that knowledge.
